Frequently Asked Questions

How does it operate?

Our methods for account recovery are diverse and tailored to each individual case. For password retrieval, we guide clients through the process of extracting an encrypted private key backup and compiling a detailed list of potential password guesses. Subsequently, we transfer this data to highly secure servers dedicated to password cracking. These servers systematically test billions or even trillions of password combinations derived from the provided data until the correct password is identified.

Can we be trusted?

When it comes to entrusting your valuable financial information, trust is paramount. At Rykalaban.com, trust forms the cornerstone of our relationship with clients. Here's why you can confidently rely on us:
We prioritize the security and confidentiality of your information. Our processes adhere to stringent security protocols, ensuring that your sensitive financial data is handled with the utmost care and protection. From encrypted communications to secure data storage, we employ industry-leading practices to safeguard your privacy.

Do I have to disclose all of my passwords to you?

Certainly. We never require our clients to disclose more information than they feel comfortable sharing with us.

What are your fees?

We operate on a service fee basis, taking 20% of any funds successfully recovered. If your account yields no funds, you won't incur any charges.

Is it possible for you to retrieve scammed or stolen assets?

Regrettably, recovering funds lost to a crypto scam is often highly improbable. However, we provide scam tracing services to assist those affected by scams in exploring potential recovery options. Our team of investigative specialists will meticulously document the trajectory of your funds and establish connections to real-world entities. Armed with this information, we'll guide you through the recommended recovery process, involving law enforcement and relevant exchanges, to maximize your chances of recuperation. We have published an article outlining the recommended steps to take if you have fallen victim to a scam.
